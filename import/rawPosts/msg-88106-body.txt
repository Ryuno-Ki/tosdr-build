You can file a claim in any state. Most states, given that the "choice of law" provision selects California law, will apply California law.

For example, if I filed a claim against GitHub in Virginia, the Virginia court would hear the case but apply California law.

See the following Wikipedia article for more information on choice of law provisions: https://secure.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/wiki/Choice_of_law_clause
